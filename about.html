ABOUT

Welcome to ARDS Bakery, where technology meets taste! We are four passionate computer science students from Tufts University who came together to combine our love for coding and baking. Our mission is simple: to bring fresh, delicious baked goods straight to your doorstep through a seamless subscription-based service.

Our Story

It all started with a late-night coding session, a box of stale cookies, and an idea—what if we could use our technical skills to make high-quality, homemade treats more accessible? From that moment, ARDS Bakery was born. We designed an intuitive platform that makes it easy for you to enjoy freshly baked goods on a regular basis, all without lifting a finger.

How It Works

Our subscription model allows you to choose your favorite baked goods, set your delivery schedule, and enjoy hassle-free indulgence. We handle everything—from sourcing quality ingredients to baking with care and delivering straight to your door. We also sell our baked goods on an individual rotating basis - make sure to regularly check our shop for new and exciting pastries!

Join the Community

At ARDS Bakery, we believe in the power of good food and great technology. Whether you're a sweet tooth, a busy professional, or someone who just loves a warm cookie with their coffee, we've got something for you. Subscribe today and experience the perfect blend of baking and innovation!
